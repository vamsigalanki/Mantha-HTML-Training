Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He holds numerous records and is the only player to have scored one hundred international centuries.

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is known for his aggressive batting style and consistent performance. He has been one of the leading run-scorers in modern cricket and has numerous records to his name.

MS Dhoni

MS Dhoni is renowned for his calm and composed demeanor on the field. He is one of the most successful captains in cricket history, having led India to numerous victories, including the ICC World Cup in 2011.

Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is known for his ability to score big hundreds and double centuries in limited-overs cricket. He is the only player to have scored three double centuries in ODIs.
